The www.ensaiosclinicos.gov.br/rg/RBR-2k58f5h/ identifier is RBR- 2k58f5h. Chronic low back pain means pain in the lower back that does not go away for more than three months. It is very common and often makes daily life harder. Physiotherapists use many treatments to help. One of them is shortwave diathermy. This treatment uses heat to try to ease pain. In this study, people with chronic low back pain tried this treatment one time. They were split into three groups. One group received steady heat. One group received heat in pulses. One group had no heat (placebo). We measured pain, movement, leg strength, flexibility, and how happy people were with the treatment. We checked these before the session, right after, and one week later. The groups that received heat felt less pain than the placebo group. The steady heat group became more flexible. The pulsed heat group became stronger. In short, one session of shortwave diathermy reduced pain right away. Each type of heat also gave extra benefits.
